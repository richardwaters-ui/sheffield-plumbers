Common Questions

Got questions? Here's the quick answers.

How much does a boiler repair cost in Sheffield?
Common repairs like diverter valves or PCBs cost £150-£300 across Sheffield and surrounding areas. Pressure sensors and thermistors are £80-£150. Full heat exchanger replacements can be £400-£600. We diagnose first (free with repair) and quote before we start any work.
How long does a boiler repair take?
Most repairs take within 2 hours once we've diagnosed the fault. Simple fixes like pressure sensors or thermostats can be done in 30 minutes. Heat exchanger replacements or complex PCB issues can take 3-4 hours. We carry common parts on the van, whether you're in City Centre, Hillsborough, or Ecclesall.
Should I repair or replace my boiler?
If your boiler is under 10 years old and the repair costs less than £400, repair it. If it's over 15 years old or the repair is expensive, replacement makes more sense. We'll advise honestly, we don't push sales.
What's the most common boiler fault?
Faulty diverter valves in combi boilers, you get heating but no hot water, or vice versa. It's a straightforward replacement taking within 2 hours and costing £150-£250. Second most common is low pressure (F1/F22 fault codes), usually fixed by repressurising the system.
Can you fix all boiler brands?
Yes, we're Gas Safe registered and trained on all major brands: Vaillant, Worcester Bosch, Ideal, Baxi, Glow-worm, and more. We carry common parts for popular models and can source specialist parts same-day or next-day.
My boiler keeps losing pressure, what's wrong?
Either there's a leak somewhere in the system (pipes, radiators, boiler heat exchanger) or the pressure relief valve is faulty and venting water outside. We'll pressure-test the system to find out which. Small leaks are often under floors or behind radiators.
What do boiler fault codes mean?
Fault codes tell us what's wrong. Common ones: F1/F22 (low pressure), EA (ignition failure), F75 (pressure sensor fault), F28 (no gas). We decode them all and know which parts fail for each code. Note the code when you call us, it speeds diagnosis.
Is a boiler service the same as a repair?
No. A service is preventative maintenance, we check everything works safely and efficiently. A repair fixes something that's already broken. If your boiler has stopped working, you need a repair. Annual services help prevent repairs.
