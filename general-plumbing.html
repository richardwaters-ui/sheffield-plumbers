Common Questions

Got questions? Here's the quick answers.

How much do general plumbing services cost in Sheffield?
Simple jobs like tap repairs cost £60-£90 across Sheffield and surrounding areas. Tap installations are £75-£120. Shower installations are £180-£350. Sink installations are £100-£200. Stopcock replacements are £120-£200. We quote upfront before starting any work, no hidden charges.
How long does a typical plumbing job take?
Tap installations take within 2 hours. Shower installations take 3-4 hours depending on the type. Sink installations take 2-3 hours. Stopcock replacements take within 2 hours. We'll give you a realistic timeframe when we assess the job, whether you're in City Centre, West Bridgford, or Beeston.
Can you fit taps and sinks I've bought myself?
Yes, we'll fit customer-supplied taps, sinks, and showers. Just make sure they're the right size and type for your setup. If there are compatibility issues, we'll advise on what you need before starting.
Do I need Gas Safe registration for a shower installation?
Only if it's a gas-powered instant water heater, which are rare now. Electric showers need a qualified electrician (we can arrange that). Regular mixer showers fed from your boiler or cylinder are straightforward plumbing, no Gas Safe needed.
Can you install an outside tap in winter?
Yes, but we'll recommend a frost-proof tap and proper insulation if the pipe runs through an unheated space. Outside taps can freeze and burst in cold weather if they're not protected properly.
How do I know if my stopcock needs replacing?
If it's stiff, won't turn all the way, or leaks when you try to use it, it needs replacing. Stopcocks in older Sheffield properties often seize from lack of use. Replacing it before an emergency is sensible.
Can you connect a washing machine or dishwasher?
Yes, we install hot and cold supplies, waste connections, and check everything for leaks. If you don't have an existing supply nearby, we can run new pipes. Most washing machine installs take within 2 hours.
What's included in re-piping work?
Re-piping means replacing old or damaged pipework with new copper or plastic pipe. This might be a single section that's corroded, or an entire run if multiple sections are failing. We assess the pipe condition and recommend the minimum work needed.
Do you handle bathroom installations?
We do the plumbing side, fitting baths, sinks, showers, and toilets. For full bathroom renovations including tiling and electrics, we can work alongside your builder or recommend trusted contractors.
