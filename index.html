Common Questions

Got questions? Here's the quick answers.

How quickly can you get to me in Sheffield?
For emergencies like burst pipes or no heating in winter, we aim to have an engineer with you within 2 hours when you call during our business hours (Mon-Thurs 8:30am-6pm, Fri 9am-4pm). Our engineers are based locally in Ecclesall, Hillsborough, and Woodseats, so we're never far away.
Do you charge a call-out fee?
No call-out fee. Our visit includes the first hour's labour when you book with us. We'll assess the problem, quote you for the work, and you only pay for repairs you agree to. Most emergency repairs in Sheffield cost £89-£400 including parts and labour.
What areas of Sheffield do you cover?
We cover all Sheffield areas including Ecclesall, Hillsborough, Woodseats, Crookes, Dore, Heeley, Broomhill, Totley, Walkley, Nether Edge, Fulwood, and Meadowhall. Our engineers are based locally across S2, S6, S7, S8, S10, S11, and S17 postcodes.
Are your plumbers Gas Safe registered?
All our engineers who work on gas appliances are Gas Safe registered where required. We carry ID cards and our Gas Safe number is visible on the van. For non-gas work like blocked drains or leaking taps, Gas Safe registration isn't required.
What if you can't fix the problem on the first visit?
We carry common parts on the van so most repairs are completed on the first visit. If we need a specialist part or further investigation, we'll make the situation safe, quote you for the full repair, and book a return visit. You only pay for work completed.
Is your work guaranteed?
Our 12-month guarantee is included with our membership plan. Members get £50 off all services, discounts on parts, £80 off our appliance store, and priority booking. Membership costs a low monthly price and gives you peace of mind on all plumbing work.
Do you work evenings and weekends?
Our phone lines are open Mon-Thurs 8:30am-6pm, Fri 9am-4pm. If you call during these hours with an emergency, we can dispatch an engineer the same day, including outside normal hours if needed. We don't answer calls on weekends but you can book ahead.
How much does a typical plumbing job cost in Sheffield?
Emergency callouts cost £89-£150. Burst pipe repairs cost £100-£400. Boiler repairs cost £120-£350. Blocked drains cost £95-£180. Tap replacement costs £60-£120. Toilet repairs cost £75-£180. We quote before starting work so you know exactly what you'll pay.
Who actually does the work?
Either one of our own employed plumbers or an approved subcontractor in your area. All are held to the same high standards, ranked by internal performance metrics and customer feedback. You'll get a local engineer who knows Sheffield properties.
Are your plumbers insured?
Yes. Every repair we carry out is fully insured, protecting your home and giving you complete peace of mind. Our engineers are employed or vetted subcontractors, and all work comes with a 12-month guarantee on repairs.
Are your plumbers vetted and qualified?
All our engineers are either directly employed or thoroughly vetted subcontractors. Every engineer is DBS checked and qualified.
How quickly can you get a plumber to me in Sheffield?
We aim to get an engineer to you as quickly as possible. Call us on 0114 303 4567 — our phone lines are open Mon-Thurs 8:30am-6pm and Fri 9am-4pm, and we can often deploy engineers outside those hours for emergencies.
What areas do you cover in Sheffield?
We cover all Sheffield areas and surrounding postcodes. Call us to check if we serve your specific location — we cover a wide area.
Do you handle genuine plumbing emergencies?
We treat all plumbing calls as emergencies. Whether it's a burst pipe, flooding, boiler breakdown or blocked drain — call us and we will respond.
